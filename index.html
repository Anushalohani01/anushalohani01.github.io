About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Anusha Lohani, an enthusiastic researcher with a strong passion for GIS, remote sensing, and data analysis using Python and R. Growing up in Nepal, I was captivated by nature’s beauty and the intricate balance of the environment, which led me to explore plant science and environmental research.

I have a background in forest ecosystem research, focusing on tree diversity, regeneration, and carbon sequestration. My work integrates geospatial technologies to analyze landscapes, monitor ecological changes, and apply data-driven solutions to real-world environmental challenges.

As I continue my journey, I aim to expand my expertise into geosciences and environmental studies, merging scientific research with cutting-edge technology to contribute to sustainability and conservation efforts. And now my childhood passion has evolved into a lifelong mission to explore, innovate, and make a meaningful impact in the world.
